For nearly 40 years Hayao Miyazaki has enchanted audiences thanks to his hand drawn lushly colored and emotionally soaring animated films. Miyazaki’s films and art have not only inspired young people throughout the world to want to learn more about japanese popular culture, they’ve helped us to dream of the past and to connect our human mythologies and cultural roots to the future.
